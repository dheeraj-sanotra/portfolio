About

Engineering backends that scale and ship

I'm a Senior Development Engineer with 8+ years of end-to-end ownership of backend systems at WishACloud (formerly AppMySite) — a SaaS mobile app builder serving 800K+ users and powering 500K+ apps with 100M+ downloads.

I own systems end-to-end: from architecture decisions and schema design to production reliability, release automation, and post-deployment monitoring. I led the migration of high-concurrency mobile APIs from Laravel to Node.js, achieving 100–300 ms response times under peak load.

I believe great backend engineering is invisible — it keeps the product fast, secure, and resilient without the user ever noticing. I build exactly that.
